# Imprint

The portal visualize.admin.ch allows the visualization of Swiss Open Government Data provided by the LINDAS Linked Data Service. Open Government Data (OGD) are data that are made available to the public free of charge in computer-readable format. For environmental data, the legal basis is Article 10e of the Environmental Protection Act.

Publishing and content: Federal Office for the Environment, 3003 Bern
